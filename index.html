Bangalore Wedding Reception 7PM onwards

The South Indian Roman Catholic Church wedding reception is a festive celebration held post-ceremony. Hosted in a banquet hall, it features traditional cuisine, and joyous festivities. The couple partakes in rituals like cake-cutting, symbolizing their union, surrounded by family and friends who offer blessings and well-wishes.

Venue: St. Thomas Parish Hall Dharmaram, Bengaluru

21st April

Pondicherry Church Wedding 11:00AM - 12:00PM

A South Indian Roman Catholic Church wedding combines Roman Catholic traditions with South Indian cultural elements. The ceremony, held in a church, includes the exchange of vows, rings, Mass, and the tying of the thali, symbolizing marital unity. The bride wears a white gown, the groom a suit, and cultural touches are woven into the celebration.

Venue: St. Andrew's Church, Reddiarpalayam, Puducherry
